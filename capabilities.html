Precision Metal Stamping

Since 1970 we have been producing high quality stampings. We are able to work with all manner of dies from large progressive tooling with high-quantity runs to small single-quantity runs. Carmeco can manufacture your parts on time and efficiently.

  • - 24 Presses
  • - Up To 1200 Tons Of Stamping Power
  • - 60,000 sq.ft Dedicated To Stamping
  • - Bed Sizes Up To 63″x 157″
  • - High-Speed Dallas & COE Servo Feeders As Well As Spindle & Air Feeders

Laser Cutting

Our laser enables Carmeco to provide its customers with high-quality, economical, and time-sensitive sheet metal production requests for OEM market.

  • - 60″ (1524mm) x 120″ (3048mm) Table
  • - Cut Mild Steel Up To 1″ (25mm)
  • - Stainless Steel Up To 0.500″ (12mm)
  • - Aluminum Up To 0.312″ (8mm)
  • - 5000 Watts Of Cutting Power
  • - Nitrogen Or Oxygen Cut Edges

Custom Welding

We can accommodate any welding job, ensuring your part is on time and to specifications. We offer all types of welding and have experience in many different weld applications. We can address your welding needs if you need one part welded or and entire assembly. Carmeco has a full line of secondary operations. Our welding procedures are patterned to AWS standards and Carmeco can offer certified welding should you require it.

  • - 20,000 sq ft. facility Dedicated To Welding
  • - Experienced Workmanship To Get The Job Done Right!
  • - MIG Welding
  • - TIG Welding
  • - Plasma Cutter
  • - Weld Fixtures

Tooling

Carmeco offers complete tooling capabilities. From simple blanks to complex progressive dies. We can keep your project on time and on budget.   • - In-House Tooling
  • - Precision Tooling Capabilities
  • - Quality Tooling Every Time
  • - Progressive Dies
  • - Over 80 Years Of Combined Experience!
