Carmeco's Green Initiatives

Last Year We Recyled 7 Tons of Scrap Metal!

Carmeco Recycling Philosophy

Although some would not imagine recycling and metal stamping go hand in hand, we here at Carmeco think it is necessary for all walks of life. Any item that can be recycled or re-purposed means less waste in our landfills.

Current Recycling Processes

  • ✓ Steel, stainless steel, and aluminum scrap is placed in a container and then taken to a metal processing center. This can be all parts or drop material that comes from production.
  • ✓ Cardboard boxes are purchased used and then any that are deemed out of service are collected. These are then picked up on a regular basis by a local nonprofit organization.

Ways We Reduce Waste

✓ We encourage e-mails instead of faxes

✓ Saving paper by utilizing our computer to access schedules

Reuse

  • ✓ All pallets/skids are purchased from a local business that rebuilds them. Old pallets are broken down and then the proper parts are used to make them sturdy again.
  • ✓ All incoming broken pallets are placed into an area for processing by a Carmeco employee who breaks them down and makes them into 4x4 lumber that is used in shipping parts out. This lowers the amount of lumber we have to discard.

Repurposing Efforts

Not only do we here at Carmeco make every effort to recycle our waste we also look for inventive ways to repurpose materials. Such as taking a top of a out of commissioned press and turning it into a desk. Also using extra metal to create racks for our warehouse. Giving new life to something that would have been disposed of while adding character and saving cost.
