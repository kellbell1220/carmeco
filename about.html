About Carmeco

Since 1970, we have been producing quality stamping and metal fabrications for original equipment manufacturers from every industry. Metal Stamping is our passion and we are driven to help your business succeed. We manufacture for some of the biggest names in the industry. Lets us help you with your metal fabrication production. Here at Carmeco we value our customers and we strive to meet there needs in any way possible.

Our Facility

- Close Proximity To I-44

- Located Between St. Louis And Springfield

- 98,000 Square Foot Facility

- 44,000 Square Foot Dedicated To Stamping

- 17 Stamping Presses Ranging From 1200 Tons To 30 Tons

- 4 Brake Presses And A Laser Cutter

Carmeco History

From our beginnings of a single stamping press in the middle of a field. To a state-of-the-art facility to producing millions of products every month. Our tenaciousness for growth and improvement has helped us secure a place among the leading metal fabrication companies in the country.

The Press That Started It All

This press started it's life as a munition press owned by Ford Motor Company during WWII. Our founder Kenneth Carr purchased it in 1970. Sparking the start of our 49 years of business.
